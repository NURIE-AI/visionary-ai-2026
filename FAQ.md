# Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Welcome to the Visionary AI: NURIE.AI 2026 Cross-Platform Innovation Awards! Here are some common questions you might have about participating in the program:

**Q1: Do I need to form a team to participate?**  
**A:** No. This competition is structured as an individual game because AI significantly streamlines the development process. However, if you do have people helping you in the background, that is perfectly fine. We view the primary registrant as the "representative person" and do not restrict how many people are in the actual team behind the scenes.

**Q2: I live outside of Taiwan and Korea. Can I still join?**  
**A:** At this time, the competition is strictly open to individuals currently residing in Taiwan or Korea. Additionally, participants must be over 18 years of age so that we can legally and logistically deliver the rewards.

**Q3: How do I get access to the VaultSage API?**  
**A:** You can obtain your Server-side API Key by heading directly to [VaultSage](https://vaultsage.ai/). You must leverage this API in your application for it to be eligible. 

**Q4: Does my application have to be a specific platform type?**  
**A:** We encourage cross-platform innovation! You are welcome to build a Web App, Mobile App, Browser Extension, or Desktop Application—whichever best showcases your creativity and technical execution.

**Q5: What are the rules for the Social Demo requirement?**  
**A:** To successfully submit your project, you must record a demo video of your application in action. This video needs to be uploaded publicly to either **YouTube** or **Instagram Reels**, and you must include the mandatory hashtag `#vaultsage` in the title or description.

**Q6: What happens if I forget to register by April 30?**  
**A:** We require participants to register by April 30 so we can understand the scale of the event and plan our resources accordingly. If you have missed this date, please reach out to the organizing team, though your final project submission must still be in by May 25.

**Q7: How will my project be judged?**  
**A:** Our judges will evaluate submissions based on three main pillars:
- **Technical Execution (45%):** System stability, feature completeness, and secure implementations.
- **Creativity (40%):** How uniquely and innovatively you apply the VaultSage API.
- **Business Potential (15%):** The project's scalability and future commercial viability.

**Q8: Can I do "vibe coding" or use AI tools to write code and build my project?**  
**A:** Yes, absolutely! We highly encourage you to use AI tools and "vibe coding" to streamline your development process. As long as your application utilizes the VaultSage backend access via your API key, you are completely free to build your project using AI assistants.
