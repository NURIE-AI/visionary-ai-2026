# Introduction to NURIE.AI

## What We Do
NURIE.AI is an innovative AI startup dedicated to the belief that work doesn't need *more* tools—it needs *better systems*. Our overarching goal is to build AI-native platforms that eliminate friction, allowing teams to act faster and focus on creating impact rather than battling "busywork." 

By seamlessly integrating specialized systems, NURIE.AI empowers businesses to quickly shift from raw inputs to clear next steps. Our core product lineup includes:

- **VaultSage:** An AI-native document intelligence drive. Stop digging for files; VaultSage connects all your documents and lets you search and ask questions across them in one centralized place.
- **BriefingHub:** A real-time market radar. Automatically track the news, signals, and trends that matter to your business, filtering out the noise.
- **MediaSage:** A multi-model visual creation studio. Generate, compare, and refine brilliant visuals across multiple Vision-Language Models (VLMs) in a single, streamlined workspace.

You can learn more about our philosophy and systems at [NURIE.AI](https://nurie.ai/en).
